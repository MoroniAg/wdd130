Welcome to Quito

Quito, the capital of Ecuador, is nestled high in the Andes at an altitude of 2,850 meters. It is renowned for its well-preserved colonial center, vibrant culture, and stunning mountain views.

The city offers a unique blend of history and modernity, with beautiful churches, lively plazas, and a rich culinary scene. Quito is also a gateway to the equator and many natural wonders of Ecuador.
